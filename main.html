About Us

Rooted in Tradition, Engineered for the Future

Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science (MITS) is promoted by Muthoot M. George Institute of Technology, a Section 25 Company within the Muthoot Group.

MITS is a self-financing technical institution offering postgraduate and undergraduate engineering programs, situated in the industrial suburb of Kochi. We uphold the standard of education that we continue to provide and nurture our students to become excellent engineers.
